Carrer

Serhat “SD” Durmus Elektronik is a TRAP Music Producer and DJ. He started his music career at the age of 12 with his friends as a drummer, and his being affected by the harmony with the drum in a short time brought him one step closer to music. At the age of 13, Elektronik was interested in music. He started to prepare Remixes and Sets with DJ programs in computer environment. While his peers were playing games, he was spending long hours or even nights at the computer, and as the days passed, he continued to grow with the pleasure of music and the love of production in him. It has been 2 years since Remix works that are not interrupted. It was time to meet Serhat Durmus's "Music Production Program", which changed the whole life course of FL Studio. In this production and the production that the program gave him, he started to focus his attention on Electronic Music with his freedom and continued his production with great determination and patience until the morning lights. Today, Serhat Durmus aims to exhibit world-class performances by crowning the music he devoted his life with concerts, embracing his lovers and accelerating the performing arts. He continues to spend his time from his young ages to this day by listening to different music types and examining them technically.
